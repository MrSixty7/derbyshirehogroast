Welcome! To the Derbyshire Hogroast Company

We are a family run business offering catering for a wide range of events from Festivals and Weddings to Parties and BBQ's.
Our catering packages include local spit roasted hog cobs with crackling, stuffing and apple sauce with the options to include many extras including roast potatoes, sausages, salad and hot and cold drinks.
We can cater at any venue or outside from our marquee.
